# Ship Power Yielding Register

## Scope

This register records the current limits and minimum requirements of power generation and power methodology for any Terran star ship wishing to exceed Lunar Orbit. It is written for inventory, mass accounting and the various technologies required on board to be approved for a contract permit. This is not a supplier specification, only a registration document detailing the requirements for being approved for a contract permit and prior to being issued a newly made or retired ship for travel.

## 0.1 Technological Baseline Rule

Values in this manifest represent minimum functional capability baselines for one full core generation structure and its scaled reactor-class applications. Specifying, installing, or configuring core systems below these operational outputs triggers an immediate regulatory compliance review.
Installed capability is not engineering layout. Engineering layout may exceed installed baselines due to auxiliary safety margins, sensor redundancies, localized shielding, structural brackets, or strategic power routing requirements.

## 0.2 Operational Regulatory Scope

This manifest defines minimum operational outputs and system baselines for engineering permitting, tech-compliance auditing, and reactor hazard mitigation. It does not dictate design layouts, manufacturing component origins, architectural routing, or creative structural configurations. The manifest is a system baseline pass-sheet. It exists solely to identify inadequate technological profiles against the current accepted safety and power law basis.
